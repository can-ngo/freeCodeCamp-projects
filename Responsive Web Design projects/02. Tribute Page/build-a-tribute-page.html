Pope Francis is the head of Catholic Church, the bishop of Rome and sovereign of Vatican City State.

Here is the timeline of Pope Francis:

• 1936 - Pope Francis was born as Jorge Mario Bergoglio in Flores.

• 1958 - After recovering from severe illness of pneumonia and cyst, He was inspired to join the Jesuit.

• 1969 - He was ordained a Catholic Priest.
